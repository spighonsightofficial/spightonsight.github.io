FAQs.

The most commonly asked questions. Don't see your question in the list? No worries. Contact me and I will respond ASAP.

Sessions range from 1 hour to 3 hours. For most clients, the typical session is roughly 2 hours. If more time is needed, arrangements can be made 2 days before the scheduled session. Contact me to discuss more details.

I will travel to the greater Nashville area for meetups. For sesions that require travel, overall price will be the total price of the session + traveling fee. Contact me if you have any questions.

Unlike other studios, I will not hold your stems. After each session, I will begin compiling your stems so they can be sent to you within the same week.

On top of individual services such as mixing, producing, and tracking, I also offer packages. Checkout the pricing section to see which packages are available currently. Don't see what you're looking for? Contact me about creating custom packages.

Policies.

Every studio has an unique atmosphere.


The atmosphere is created by the studio's mission, morals, and values. Being a faith-based studio, I do ask clients to please follow these policies during sessions.

Entourage.

Due to limited room capacity, the client can only bring 2 others during a session.

Content.

I do not support topics containing: excessive cussing, drugs, gun violence, and womanizing.

Environment.

To maintain a clean environment, clients and client entourage are prohibited from smoking while in the home studio.
